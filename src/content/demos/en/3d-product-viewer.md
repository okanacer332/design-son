## 3D Experience in Browser

This demo leverages the power of WebGL technology to offer a 360-degree examination of your products. It works without requiring any plugins.

### Technical Details
* **Three.js:** 3D render engine.
* **React Three Fiber:** 3D scene management with React component structure.
* **Drei:** Ready-made camera controls and environment lighting.
* **GLTF/GLB:** Optimized 3D model format.

With this technology, your customers can virtually "pick up" and examine the product.
