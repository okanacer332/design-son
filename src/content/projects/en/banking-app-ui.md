## Future of Finance

We reinterpreted the traditional banking experience according to the expectations of Gen Z and modern users. We reduced transaction steps by 30% without compromising security.

### Design Approach
* **Biometric Authentication:** Fast login with Face ID.
* **One-Handed Use:** Thumb-focused navigation structure.
* **Personalization:** Homepage that adapts to user spending habits.
